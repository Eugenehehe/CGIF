UFIT Inventory Evidence Workbench

Prototype boundary Synthetic/anonymized data only. Do not upload real UFIT serials, bins, ticket IDs, or internal records.
Cross-system IT asset governance

Flowtrac may be the inventory system of record, but its data can become stale when receiving, scanning, deployment, or bin transportation evidence breaks.

CGIF-ITAM reconciles Flowtrac against vendor, receiving scan, warehouse, technician deployment, and bin-ticket evidence. It shows what matches, what conflicts, what evidence is missing, and who should review next.

What problem this solves

1. Flowtrac can be stale

A device may physically move from warehouse to building, but Flowtrac bin may not update if the bin transportation ticket is missing or delayed.

2. Serial numbers can conflict

Vendor, scanner spreadsheet, and Flowtrac records may disagree because of scanner, import, barcode, or formatting issues.

3. Manual audit is slow

Student ops or DCL staff may spend time manually checking Excel files without a consistent review rationale or audit receipt.

Before CGIF-ITAM

  1. UF department sends RFQ ticket to DCL.
  2. DCL requests quotes from vendors.
  3. Department chooses vendor and order is made.
  4. Vendor ships items to warehouse.
  5. Warehouse receives and scans items.
  6. Script/spreadsheet prepares Flowtrac records.
  7. Technician deploys device.
  8. Technician should send bin transportation ticket.
  9. Flowtrac bin should update.
  10. If any step fails, Megan/student ops manually audits mismatches.

After CGIF-ITAM

  1. Upload Flowtrac export, vendor source, scan spreadsheet, and ticket evidence.
  2. Normalize serials and compare sources.
  3. Detect stale bins, serial conflicts, missing tickets, and ready updates.
  4. Route each exception to the right reviewer.
  5. Reviewer approves, overrides, or requests evidence.
  6. Generate update preview and audit receipt.

Evidence Boundary

CGIF-ITAM should not treat an inferred bin, DNS clue, or missing ticket as final physical truth. It separates proposed updates from approved updates.
